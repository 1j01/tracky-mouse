Tracky Mouse Privacy Policy

All processing is done locally on your computer.

Tracky Mouse does not store any personally identifiable information.

When an error occurs, the Tracky Mouse desktop app may send error reports to a third-party service (Sentry) to help with improving the software. No personally identifiable information is collected, only stack traces and environment details, such as CPU type, operating system version, and app version.
